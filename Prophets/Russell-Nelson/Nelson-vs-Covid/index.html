    Estimated time to read: 17 minutes

    If ever there was an opportunity for God to prove to the entire world that He has a living spokesperson on Earth—Russell M Nelson, a prophet, seer, revelator, one who can see around corners, help guide the faithful, etc—this was it. We've got more practicing, faithful LDS tuning in live to hear the prophet's divine wisdom than at any prior time, expecting leaders to share some insights, words of warning, assurance if it were nothing to fear, advice to prepare, anything would be a great chance to show that this church is led by God Himself.

    What do we get instead?
