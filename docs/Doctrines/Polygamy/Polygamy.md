There exists documentary evidence that later church leaders (Young, Taylor, etc) retrofitted Joseph‚Äôs revelations like [[DC 132|D&C 132]] to endorse polygamous behavior post-mortem.

We can banter about that until we‚Äôre blue in the face, as there is a multiplicity of well-sourced information to support both sides of that argument. That dilemma will be further explored in the note section on the Succession Crisis... once I gather enough information to write about it.
